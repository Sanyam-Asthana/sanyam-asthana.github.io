#Introduction

My name is **Sanyam Asthana**, and I am a dual-degree student of **Computational Linguistics** at **IIIT Hyderabad**.

I have a keen interest in machine learning and natural language processing, along with the underlying architecture which powers it. I also like to take part in CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions and am consequently interested in cybersecurity and related tooling.

Recently I have been interested in low-level systems programming, whether it be making kernel userlands, programming languages, or full-blown virtual machines themselves.

I have a peculiar interest in learning about programming languages, both popular and obscure, with my go-to languages being Java, C, Python and Go.
